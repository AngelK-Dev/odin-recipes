Shawarma Recipe

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Lettuce
  • 1 cup Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 Cup Ketchup
  • 1/4 Cup Sweet chilli sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons Suya spice
  • 600g chicken
  • 1/2 Tbs garlic
  • 1/2 Tbs ginger
  • 1/2 Tbs paprika
  • 1/2 Tbs italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp Curry powder
  • 1/2 Tsp adobo
  • 1 cube chicken seasoning
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Slice the cabbage, carrot and lettuce.
  2. Mix your mayonnaise, ketchup, sweet chilli sauce and suya spice.
  3. Cut your chicken into pieces and add your spices and a little bit of vegetable oil
  4. Make sure the chicken is well coated
  5. Heat up your oil in a pan
  6. Fry your chicken pieces, make sure it's not crowded because you want to fry them.Then set the chicken aside
  7. In a big bowl, combine the vegetables, sauce mix and the chicken pieces. MKae sure you mix it thoroughly
  8. Coat your wrap with the sauce and add the chicken and vegetables
  9. Roll the wrap and place it on a pan to heat it up.
